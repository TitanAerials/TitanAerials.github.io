F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are the benefits of using a drone for real estate photography?

    Drones provide a unique perspective and can capture aerial shots that traditional photography methods can’t. This allows for a more comprehensive view of the property and its surroundings. Additionally, drone photography can help showcase the property in a way that is both visually appealing and informative.

  • What kind of equipment do you use for real estate drone photography?

    Our company uses state-of-the-art drones equipped with high-resolution cameras, gimbal stabilization systems, and GPS technology. We use drones from well-known brands such as DJI, ensuring that the footage is of the highest quality.

  • Can you provide interior as well as exterior shots of a property?

    Yes, we offer both interior and exterior photography services. Our drones are equipped with high-quality cameras that can capture both aerial shots and interior shots of a property.

  • How do you ensure the safety of the property during the photoshoot?

    Safety is our top priority. Our pilots are trained and experienced, and they follow strict safety protocols to ensure the property and its surroundings are not damaged during the photoshoot.

  • What is the turn-around time for the completed photos and videos?

    The turn-around time for completed photos and videos depends on the scope of the project. However, we typically provide our clients with the finished product within 2 to 3 business days of the photoshoot.

  • How much do you charge for drone photography services?

    The cost of our drone photography services depends on several factors, including the size of the property, the complexity of the shoot, and the amount of editing required. We provide customized quotes to meet the specific needs of each project, and we are transparent about our pricing from the start. Contact us for a quote and to discuss your project in detail.
